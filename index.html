Terms & Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using the TechFix website, you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our platform.

2. Product Purchases

All products listed are subject to availability. Prices are in Nigerian Naira (₦) inclusive of applicable taxes. Orders are confirmed only upon successful payment processing.

3. Repair Services

By submitting a repair booking, you authorise TechFix to inspect and repair your device. A quote will be provided before any work commences. A 30-day warranty applies to all repair work.

4. Payment

All payments are processed securely through Paystack. We accept card payments, bank transfers, and USSD. TechFix does not store your payment card details.

5. Delivery Policy

We offer same-day delivery within Lagos for orders placed before 12pm on business days. Nationwide delivery takes 3–5 business days. Delivery fees vary by location and are shown at checkout.

6. Returns & Refunds

Products may be returned within 7 days of delivery if unused and in original packaging. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days. Repair fees are non-refundable once work has commenced.

7. Privacy Policy

We collect personal information solely for processing orders and repair bookings. We do not sell or share your data with third parties without consent. You may request data deletion at any time by contacting support@techfix.ng.

8. Contact

For any questions: legal@techfix.ng
