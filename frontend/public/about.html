About Us

Who We Are

TechFix is Nigeria's most trusted electronics sales and repair centre — built on transparency, expertise, and customer satisfaction.

Our Story

Founded in 2019, TechFix started as a small phone repair outlet in Lagos. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service electronics company — offering retail sales, professional device repairs, and digital support to thousands of customers across Nigeria.

Our mission is simple: make technology accessible, affordable, and reliable for every Nigerian.
